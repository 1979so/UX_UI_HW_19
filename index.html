My UX SKills

Proficant in Figma prototyping

As a professional UX/UI designer, I rely on Figma as my primary design tool due to its unparalleled versatility and collaborative capabilities. Figma's cloud-based platform enables seamless collaboration with team members and clients in real-time, allowing for instant feedback and fostering a more efficient design process. The intuitive interface and robust set of features empower me to ideate, prototype, and iterate with ease, ensuring the creation of polished and visually appealing designs. Moreover, Figma's extensive library of plugins and integrations expands my workflow possibilities, allowing me to streamline tasks and maintain a consistent design language across projects. With Figma at the core of my toolkit, I can deliver exceptional user experiences that elevate brands and connect with audiences in meaningful ways.

Invision helps me elevate my design concepts

InVision stands as an integral tool in my UX/UI design process, elevating my design concepts from static ideas to dynamic and interactive prototypes. With InVision's seamless prototyping features, I can breathe life into my designs, allowing clients and stakeholders to experience the user journey firsthand. Its user-friendly interface empowers me to swiftly create interactive mockups, fostering iterative design and gathering invaluable feedback to refine the user experience. Additionally, InVision's collaboration capabilities facilitate efficient communication with team members, ensuring a smooth and productive design workflow. Embracing InVision as a core component of my toolkit, I am equipped to deliver captivating and user-centered designs that resonate with audiences and drive the success of every project.

When in the role of Scrum Master I use Trello.

As a professional UX/UI designer, I value Trello as an indispensable tool in my design arsenal for its exceptional project management and collaboration capabilities. Trello's intuitive interface empowers me to organize and prioritize design tasks effectively, ensuring a streamlined and efficient workflow. By utilizing its visual boards, lists, and cards, I can easily track design iterations, deadlines, and progress, keeping every aspect of my projects in clear view. Trello's collaborative nature allows seamless communication with team members and stakeholders, enabling real-time feedback and fostering a cohesive design process. Its adaptability to remote work environments ensures uninterrupted collaboration regardless of geographic locations. With Trello as my project management companion, I can confidently deliver outstanding user experiences that are thoughtfully organized, iterative, and aligned with project goals and stakeholder expectations.
